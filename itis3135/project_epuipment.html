Gear & Equipment Information

Required Gear

  • 16 oz boxing gloves (RECOMMENDED)
  • 180" hand wraps (RECOMMENDED)

Optional Gear

  • Boxing shoes
  • Mouthpiece and headgear (only needed for sparring)

Rental Gear

  • Some gloves and wraps are available for new members to borrow
  • See club officers for semester-long rental options
  • Rental gear must be kept clean and returned in good condition

Recommended Shopping Links

  • Amazon – 16oz Gloves
  • Amazon – Hand Wraps
  • Amazon – Headgear
  • Amazon – Boxing Shoes

Notes

New members don’t need all their own gear to start. Just bring water and workout clothes—we’ll help you gear up as needed!
