Welcome to the Charlotte Boxing Club

Step into the ring with Charlotte Boxing Club and discover the art of boxing like never before! Our program teaches the fundamentals—footwork, punching, defense—and provides a full-body workout that boosts confidence, relieves stress, and keeps you in peak shape.

Whether you're looking to stay active, challenge yourself, or build new friendships in a supportive environment, Charlotte Boxing Club is the place to be. No experience needed!

Training Information

Location: Belk Gym – Mat Room 011/020

Meeting Times:
Monday: 4–7 pm / 8–10 pm
Wednesday: 8–10 pm

Requirements: The club provides a few gloves and wraps for newcomers. If you plan to train regularly, you should purchase your own gear: 16oz gloves and 180" hand wraps are RECOMMENDED; boxing shoes are OPTIONAL.

Membership & Registration

  • $30 membership fee (non-refundable)
  • Payment deadline: 2/17
  • Must complete DSE form to be allowed at practice
  • Hoodies and T-shirts are separate purchases

Note: All members can try sessions for the first two weeks at no cost to see if it’s the right fit!

Meet the Coaches

Coach Joseph: A seasoned boxer with 55 amateur fights, Joseph leads with discipline, motivation, and heart. He helps members grow both in the ring and personally.

Assistant Coach Jalen: Introduced through Coach Joseph, Jalen joined as assistant coach last year. With a love for the sport and the community, he supports members and brings positive energy to the club.

Training Style

Sessions include warm-ups, drills, and technique practice. Sparring is optional and requires prior coach approval for safety.

Academics First

We prioritize school. If you miss training for academics, we fully understand. Just let us know!

Club Gear & Events

  • Rental gear available—see club officers
  • Events include hiking, grilling, paddle boating, etc.
  • Hoodies and shirts coming soon (base price only)
