Welcome to the Charlotte Boxing Club

Step into the ring with Charlotte Boxing Club and discover the art of boxing like never before! Our program teaches the fundamentals—footwork, punching, defense—and provides a full-body workout that boosts confidence, relieves stress, and keeps you in peak shape.

Whether you're looking to stay active, challenge yourself, or build new friendships in a supportive environment, Charlotte Boxing Club is the place to be. No experience needed!
