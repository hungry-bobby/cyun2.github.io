Meet the Coaches

Coach Joseph

Coach Joseph is a mentor, motivator, and the instructor behind his gym community. A proud veteran and boxer since the age of 10 with 55 amateur fights, Joseph combines classic fundamentals with innovative techniques. His training emphasizes that boxing builds character as much as skill, inspiring athletes to push their limits with determination and focus. Passionate about the sport, Joseph helps fighters succeed in the ring and grow as individuals, leaving a lasting impact that extends beyond training.

Assistant Coach Jalen

Assistant Coach Jalen joined our team last winter after moving to Charlotte. Introduced through Coach Joseph’s network, Jalen brings a wealth of experience and community spirit. With a few years of boxing experience, he has been volunteering with the club out of pure passion. Always willing to help, he supports members, ensures training runs smoothly, and steps in when Coach Joseph is unavailable. Jalen plays a vital role in maintaining the club’s momentum and fostering a positive, supportive vibe for all.
