About Charlotte Boxing Club

The Charlotte Boxing Club is more than just a training space—it's a supportive community where students of all experience levels can learn, grow, and challenge themselves through the sport of boxing.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment that promotes physical fitness, mental resilience, and discipline. Whether you're completely new to boxing or a seasoned athlete, our club offers opportunities for personal growth, camaraderie, and competition.

We are a student-run organization based at UNC Charlotte, open to all enrolled students. Our coaches and officers work hard to ensure every session is productive, safe, and enjoyable. In addition to boxing fundamentals, our members gain leadership experience, event-planning skills, and lifelong friendships.

We encourage everyone to attend a session, no matter your fitness level. There are no tryouts or experience required—just bring a positive attitude and a willingness to push your limits!
