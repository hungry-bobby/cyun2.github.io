Project Overview - UNCC Boxing Club Web App

1. Project Overview

  • Purpose: To create an interactive and informative website for the UNCC Boxing Club, streamlining communications and recruitment.
  • Intended Users: UNCC students interested in joining or learning about the boxing club, alumni, and potential event attendees.
  • Content: Club information, training schedules, leadership bios, event announcements, and sign-up forms.

5. Website Pages

  1. Home: Introduces the club and links to all other pages. Audience: new visitors.
  2. Schedule: Shows training times and locations. Includes interactive calendar. No form input.
  3. Leadership: Bios and photos of club leaders. Interactive hover reveal feature.
  4. Events: Upcoming matches and volunteer opportunities. Includes RSVP form.
  5. Join Us: Sign-up form for new members. Includes validation and confirmation popup.
