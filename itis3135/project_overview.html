Project Overview – Charlotte Boxing Club Web App

1. Project Overview

  • Purpose: To create an interactive and informative website for the Charlotte Boxing Club, streamlining communications and recruitment.
  • Intended Users: Current UNCC students.
  • Content: Club info, training schedule, leadership bios, equipment list, and signup form.

2. Client Information

  • Name: Charlotte Boxing Club
  • Organization: Charlotte Boxing Club
  • Email: CharlotteBoxing@Charlotte.edu
  • Phone: 555-555-5555

3. Wireframe

Homepage wireframe – layout includes navbar, hero image, schedule grid, and footer.

4. Site Map

Site Map – Six interconnected pages accessible via the navigation bar.

5. Website Pages

  • Home: Reloads the homepage. Contains hero image and welcome message.
  • About: Informative page describing the club’s mission and background.
  • Schedule: Displays training sessions and event details in a calendar-style layout.
  • Leadership: Shows bios and photos of club leaders with hover interactivity.
  • Equipment: Provides links and information on required boxing gear (gloves, headgear, etc.).
  • Signup: New member registration form with validation and confirmation message.

6. Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: Reloads the homepage.
  • Audience: All site visitors, new and returning students.
  • Content: Charlotte Boxing image, welcome message, navbar, and footer.
  • Form Input: No
  • Validations: No
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation links in header/footer
  • Actions: no further actions other than navigation links in header/footer
  • Special Notes: Features background image and centered branding

About

  • Purpose: Describes the club’s mission, origin, and values.
  • Audience: Interested students, faculty, or prospective members
  • Content: Text and images about club history, mission, and leadership
  • Form Input: No
  • Validations: No
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation links in header/footer
  • Actions: no further actions other than navigation links in header/footer
  • Special Notes: Simple layout, potentially with animated transitions

Schedule

  • Purpose: Lists practice times, events, and meeting info
  • Audience: Club members and interested students
  • Content: list of weekly training schedule
  • Form Input: No
  • Validations: No
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation links in header/footer
  • Actions: no further actions other than navigation links in header/footer
  • Special Notes: might add advisory message if training times change due to weather or other conditions

Leadership

  • Purpose: Introduces current team leaders
  • Audience: New members
  • Content: Bios with a central group photo
  • Form Input: No
  • Validations: No
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation links in header/footer
  • Actions: no further actions other than navigation links in header/footer
  • Special Notes: purely informative

Equipment

  • Purpose: Shows required gear (headgear, wraps, gloves, etc.)
  • Audience: New members preparing to join
  • Content: List of required and recommended items with links
  • Form Input: No
  • Validations: No
  • Buttons/Links: Links to external gear sites (Amazon, etc.)
  • Actions: Opens product links
  • Special Notes: purely informative

Signup

  • Purpose: Allows new members to register
  • Audience: Interested UNCC students
  • Content: Form with name, email, and comments
  • Form Input: Yes
  • Validations: JavaScript validation + confirmation
  • Buttons/Links: Submit and reset buttons
  • Actions: Form submits and shows confirmation message
  • Special Notes: May include checkbox for consent agreement
